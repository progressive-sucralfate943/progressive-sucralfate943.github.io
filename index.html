💡 What is video-use?

video-use helps you edit video files using automated agents. You give the software instructions, and the tool performs the edits on your behalf. This approach removes the need for complex manual timelines or deep technical expertise in professional video editing software.

You can perform tasks like trimming silence, adding captions, or rearranging clips by typing language prompts. The goal is to make video production accessible for everyone, regardless of their background in digital media.

⚙️ System Requirements

To run this application effectively on your Windows computer, please ensure your system meets these basic standards:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11
  • Processor: Modern dual-core processor or better
  • Memory: 8 GB of RAM or more
  • Storage: 500 MB of free disk space for the installation
  • Graphics: Hardware supporting DirectX 11 or higher

🚀 Using the Application

After installation, find the video-use icon on your desktop or in your start menu. The interface opens to a clean dashboard. To start your first project, drag and drop your video file into the main window.

Type your request in the text box at the bottom. For example, you might type "Cut the parts where I pause" or "Add a fade transition at the two-minute mark." The agent processes your request and applies the changes to your file.

When you finish editing, click the Export button to save your new video. You can choose your preferred file format and quality settings before final processing completes.

🛡️ Security and Privacy

Your media files remain on your local computer at all times. This software does not upload your raw footage to external servers for processing. All edit commands occur within your own system's environment to protect your privacy and data security.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

If the application fails to open, ensure your Windows system is up to date. Occasionally, antivirus software might flag local applications during the first launch. If you see a security warning, verify the publisher information and click "Run anyway" if the certificate matches our verified release.

For performance boosts, close other heavy applications while working on long videos. This allows the agent to use more memory for faster processing speeds.
